What is CryptoTab Farm? +

CryptoTab Farm is a powerful tool that allows you to turn your idle computer power into Bitcoin profit. It manages your mining hardware and provides a unified dashboard for all your devices.

How do I start mining? +

Simply download the miner software for your OS (Windows/Mac), install it, and connect it to your account using the QR code in your dashboard.

What are Pool Miners? +

Pool Miners are cloud-based virtual mining machines that allow you to earn BTC without owning any physical hardware. They are perfect for users without a PC.

Is there a minimum withdrawal? +

Yes, the minimum withdrawal is 0.00001 BTC. Withdrawals are processed instantly to your wallet with zero network fees.

How much can I earn? +

Your earnings depend on your hardware power (hashrate) and the time your devices spend mining. You can increase profit by adding more miners or inviting friends.
